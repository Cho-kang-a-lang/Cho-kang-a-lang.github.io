<font size=6 color=#3F48CC>THE LAIDLY WORM</font>
<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>In Bamborough Castle there once lived a King who had two children, a son named Childe Wynde, and a daughter who was called May Margret. Their mother, a fair woman, was dead, and the King mourned her long and faithfully. But, after his son Childe Wynde went to seek his fortune, the King, hunting in the forest, came across a lady of such great beauty that he fell in love with her at once and determined to marry her.</font>
<br>

<font size=6>恐怖ê蛇龍</font>
<font size=5>Éng-kòe，tī Bián-bu̍h-lò͘城堡--ni̍h，bat有1 ê王tòa tī hia，伊有2 ê gín-á，1 ê是hāu-seⁿ，號做Chhió͘ Oén，koh有1 ê cha-bó͘-kiáⁿ號做Mé Má-koeh。In老母是1位真súi ê女性，m̄-koh已經過身，王長久以來mā lóng誠心誠意teh ka哀悼。M̄-koh to̍h tī in kiáⁿ Chhió͘ Oén出外去走chhōe伊ka-tī ê字運了後，1遍王tī大樹林á內phah獵ê時，去tú tio̍h 1位美麗到極ê女士，伊1-ē to̍h去ho͘ saⁿh--tio̍h，決心beh ka chhōa轉來做牽手。</font>
<br>

<font size=6 color=#8E403A>KHIÓNG-PÒ͘ Ê CHÔA-LÊNG</font>
<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Éng-kòe, tī Bián-bu̍h-lò͘ siâⁿ-pó--ni̍h, bat ū 1 ê ông tòa tī hia, i ū 2 ê gín-á, 1 ê sī hāu-seⁿ, hō chò Chhió͘ Oén, koh ū 1 ê cha-bó͘-kiáⁿ hō chò Mé Má-koeh. In lāu-bú sī 1 ūi chin súi ê lú-sèng, m̄-koh í-keng kòe-sin, ông tn̂g-kú í-lâi mā lóng sêng-sim-sêng-ì teh ka ai-tō. M̄-koh to̍h tī in kiáⁿ Chhió͘ Oén chhut-gōa khì cháu-chhōe i ka-tī ê jī-ūn liáu-āu, 1 piàn ông tī tōa-chhiū-nâ-á lāi phah-la̍h ê sî, khì tú tio̍h 1 uī bí-lē kàu-ke̍k ê lú-sū, i 1-ē to̍h khì ho͘ saⁿh--tio̍h, koat-sim beh ka chhōa tńg-lâi chò khan-chhiú.</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Now Princess May Margret was not over-pleased to think that her mother's place should be taken by a strange woman, nor was she pleased to think that she would have to give up keeping house for her father the King. For she had always taken a pride in her work. But she said nothing, though she stood long on the castle walls looking out across the sea wishing for her dear brother's return; for, see you, they had mothered each other.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Taⁿ，公主Mé Má-koeh nā去想tio̍h講in老母ê位置koh來ē去hō͘ 1 ê生分ê女性取代，伊to̍h無kài歡喜，koh想tio̍h伊mā tio̍h ài放棄替伊ê父王hōaⁿ家，ûn-ná mā是歡喜bē --起-來。因為做chit項khang-khòe一直hō͘伊感覺真驕傲。M̄-koh伊啥to無講，kan-na to̍h不時chiú tī城堡ê牆圍á頂khiā真久teh看海，ǹg望伊親愛ê阿兄轉--來；khah án-chóaⁿ講，in 2 ê éng過to̍h bē輸是對方ê老母án-ne teh sio照顧。</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Taⁿ, kong-chú Mé Má-koeh nā khì siūⁿ tio̍h kóng in lāu-bú ê uī-tì koh-lâi ē khì hō͘ 1 ê chheⁿ-hūn ê lú-sèng chhú-tāi, i to̍h bô kài hoaⁿ-hí, koh siūⁿ tio̍h i mā tio̍h ài hòng-khì thè i ê hū-ông hōaⁿ-ke, ûn-ná mā sī hoaⁿ-hí bē --khí-lâi. In-ūi chò chit hāng khang-khòe it-ti̍t hō͘ i kám-kak chin kiau-ngō͘. M̄-koh i siáⁿ to bô kóng, kan-na to̍h put-sî chiú tī siâⁿ-pó ê chhiûⁿ-ûi-á téng khiā chin kú teh khòaⁿ hái, ǹg-bāng i chhin-ài ê a-hiaⁿ tńg--lâi; khah án-chóaⁿ kóng, in 2 ê éng-kòe to̍h bē-su sī tùi-hong ê lāu-bú án-ne teh sio chiàu-kò͘.</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Still no news came of Childe Wynde; so on the day when the old King was to bring the new Queen home, May Margret counted over the keys of the castle chambers, knotted them on a string, and after casting them over her left shoulder for luck—more for her father's sake than for the new Queen's regard—she stood at the castle gate ready to hand over the keys to her stepmother.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>To̍h án-ne一直lóng等無Chhió͘ Oén ê消息；só͘-pái to̍h tī老王beh kā新王后chhōa轉來厝hit kang， Mé Má-koeh kā城堡內底所有房間ê鎖匙siàu好勢，用1條索á ka kat--起-來，sòa--lòe，為tio̍h beh求好字運，伊kā hit kōaⁿ鎖匙ùi伊ê tò-pêng肩胛後tàn--過-去—che是為in老爸佔khah大面，m̄是為tio̍h beh尊存新王后—了後伊to̍h khiā tī城堡ê大門口，準備好beh kā chia ê鎖匙交hō͘伊ê後母。</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>To̍h án-ne it-ti̍t lóng tán bô Chhió͘ Oén ê siau-sit; só͘-pái to̍h tī lāu  ông beh kā sin ông-hiō chhōa tńg-lâi chhù hit kang, Mé Má-koeh kā siâⁿ-pó lāi-té só͘-ū pâng-keng ê só-sî siàu hó-sè, ēng 1 tiâu soh-á ka kat--khí-lâi, sòa--lòe, ūi tio̍h beh kiû hó jī-ūn, i kā hit kōaⁿ só-sî ùi i ê tò-pêng keng-kah-āu tàn--kòe-khì — che sī ūi in lāu-pē chiàm khah tōa-bīn, m̄-sī ūi tio̍h beh chun-chhûn sin ông-hiō — liáu-āu i to̍h khiā tī siâⁿ-pó ê tōa-mn̂g-kháu, chún-pī hó beh kā chia ê só-sî kau hō͘ i ê āu-bú.</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC> Now as the bridal procession approached with all the lords of the north countrie, and some of the Scots lords in attendance, she looked so fair and so sweet, that the lords whispered to one another of her beauty. And when, after saying in a voice like a mavis—</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Tī chhōa親ê行伍beh到ê時，做夥行--ê有北pêng所有國家ê王，koh有1 kóa蘇格蘭貴族mā來參加典禮，新娘看--起-來是hiah-nī-á妖嬌美麗koh甜but-but，hō͘ hia-ê貴族lóng互相chhī-bú-chhī-chhū teh o-ló伊ê súi。Chit時，Mé Má-koeh用伊bē輸花眉hiah-nī好聽ê聲講—</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Tī chhōa-chhin ê hâng-ngó͘ beh kàu ê sî, chò-hóe kiâⁿ--ê ū pak-pêng só͘-ū kok-ka ê ông, koh ū 1 kóa So͘-kek-lân kùi-cho̍k mā lâi chham-ka tián-lé, sin-niû khòaⁿ--khí-lâi sī hiah-nī-á iau-kiau bí-lē koh tiⁿ-but-but, hō͘ hia-ê kùi-cho̍k lóng hō͘-siong chhī-bú-chhī-chhū teh o-ló i ê súi. Chit sî, Mé Má-koeh iōng i bē-su hoe-bî hiah-nī hó-thiaⁿ ê siaⁿ kóng —</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC> "Oh welcome, welcome, father,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Unto your halls and towers!</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>And welcome too, my stepmother,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>For all that's here is yours!"</font>
<br>

<font size=5>「O͘h，歡迎，歡迎，我ê父親，</font>
<br>

<font size=5>歡迎你轉來到你ê廳堂kah城樓！</font>
<br>

<font size=5>也歡迎--你，我ê後母，</font>
<br>

<font size=5>因為Chia ê一切lóng是你ê！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>“O͘h, hoan-gêng, hoan-gêng, góa ê hū-chhin,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Hoan-gêng lí tńg-lâi kàu lí ê thiaⁿ-tn̂g kah siâⁿ-lâu!</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Iā hoan-gêng--lí, góa ê āu-bú,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>In-ūi chia ê it-chhè lóng sī lí ê!”</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>she turned upon the step and tripped into the yard, the Scots lords said aloud:</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>"Forsooth! May Margret's grace</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Surpasses all that we have met, she has so fair a face!"</font>
<br>

<font size=5>伊tī gîm-á頂轉身，脚步輕ió-ió踏入去庭--ni̍h，hia ê蘇格蘭貴族大聲講：</font>
<br>

<font size=5>「有影--ê！Mé Má-koeh ê優雅，</font>
<br>

<font size=5>贏過所有goán bat看--過ê人，伊ê面容to̍h是hiah-nī súi！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>I tī gîm-á téng tńg-sin, kha-pō͘ khin-ió-ió ta̍h ji̍p-khì tiâⁿ--ni̍h, hia ê So͘-kek-lân kùi-cho̍k tōa-siaⁿ kóng:</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>“Ū-iáⁿ--ê! Mé Má-koeh ê iu-ngá,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Iâⁿ kòe só͘-ū goán bat khòaⁿ--kòe ê lâng, i ê bīn-iông to̍h sī hiah-nī súi!”</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Now the new Queen overheard this, and she stamped her foot and her face flushed with anger as she turned her about and called:</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>"You might have excepted me,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>But I will bring May Margret to a Laidly Worm's degree;</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>I'll bring her low as a Laidly Worm</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>That warps about a stone,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>And not till the Childe of Wynde come back</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Will the witching be undone."</font>
<br>

<font size=5>新王后去聽tio̍h chia ê話了後，tiô脚tǹg蹄，面á氣kah反紅，seng-khu oa̍t過來講：</font>
<br>

<font size=5>「Lín凡勢是bē記chit kā我算在內，</font>
<br>

<font size=5>M̄-koh sòa--lòe我to̍h beh kā Mé Má-koeh變kah像恐怖ê蛇龍hiah-nī bái，</font>
<br>

<font size=5>我beh hō͘伊像蛇龍hiah-nī臭賤，</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Khûn tī石頭頂，</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Nā是Chhió͘ Oén無轉--來</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Chit ê法術to̍h bē破解。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Sin ông-hiō khì thiaⁿ tio̍h chia ê ōe liáu-āu, tiô-kha-tǹg-tê, bīn-á khì kah hoán-âng, seng-khu oa̍t kòe-lâi kóng:</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>“Lín hoân-sè sī bē-kì-chit kā góa sǹg chāi-lāi,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>M̄-koh sòa--lòe góa to̍h beh kā Mé Má-koeh piàn kah chhiūⁿ Khióng-pò͘ ê chôa-lêng hiah-nī bái,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Góa beh hō͘ i chhiūⁿ chôa-lêng hiah-nī chhàu-chiān,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Khûn tī chio̍h-thâu téng,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Nā-sī Chhió͘ Oén bô tńg--lâi</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Chit ê hoat-su̍t to̍h bē phò-kái.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Well! hearing this May Margret laughed, not knowing that her new stepmother, for all her beauty, was a witch; and the laugh made the wicked woman still more angry. So that same night she left her royal bed, and, returning to the lonely cave where she had ever done her magic, she cast Princess May Margret under a spell with charms three times three, and passes nine times nine. And this was her spell:</font>
<br>

<font size=5>聽tio̍h chit kóa話，Mé Má-koeh chŏaⁿ笑--出-來，伊m̄知伊ê新後母，sui-bóng人真súi，m̄-koh soah是1 ê女術士；伊ê笑聲hō͘ chit ê惡質ê chŏ͘人更加受氣。Só͘-pái to̍h tī hit暝，王后離開伊皇室ê睏房，轉去伊iap-thiap ê山洞，也to̍h是伊éng過teh作法ê所在，伊念咒唸9遍，手舞81遍，對Mé Má-koeh公主放符á。伊ê咒語是án-ne： </font>
<br>

<font size=5>Thiaⁿ tio̍h chit kóa ōe, Mé Má-koeh chŏaⁿ chhiò--chhut-lâi, i m̄-chai i ê sin āu-bú, sui-bóng lâng chin súi, m̄-koh soah sī 1 ê lú su̍t-sū; i ê chhiò-siaⁿ hō͘ chit ê ok-chit ê chŏ͘-lâng kèng-ka siū-khì. Só͘-pái to̍h tī hit mê, ông-hiō lī-khui i hông-sek ê khùn-pâng, tńg-khì i iap-thiap ê soaⁿ-tōng, iā to̍h sī i éng-kòe teh chok-hoat ê só͘-chāi, i liām-chiù liām 9 piàn, chhiú bú 81 piàn, tùi Mé Má-koeh kong-chú pàng-hû-á. I ê chiù-gí sī án-ne:</font>
<br>

 <font size=5 color=#3F48CC>"I weird ye to a Laidly Worm,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>And such sail ye ever be</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Until Childe Wynde the King's dear son</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Comes home across the sea.</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Until the world comes to an end</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Unspelled ye'll never be,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Unless Childe Wynde of his own free will</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>Sail give you kisses three!"</font>
<br>

<font size=5>「我咒詛你變做1尾恐怖ê蛇龍，</font>
<br>

<font size=5>你ē永遠lóng生做án-ne，</font>
<br>

<font size=5>直直到Chhió͘ Oén，王親愛ê kiáⁿ兒</font>

<br>

<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>“Góa chiùchó͘ lí piàn-chò 1 bóe khióng-pò͘ ê chôa-lêng,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Lí ē éng-oán lóng seⁿ-chò án-ne,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Ti̍t-ti̍t kàu Chhió͘ Oén, ông chhin-ài ê kiáⁿ-jî</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Kòe-hái tńg-chhù.</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Ti̍t-ti̍t kàu thiⁿ-tē-bóe</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Lí to bô-tàng hòa-kái,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Tû-hui sī Chhió͘ Oén ka-tī hoaⁿ-hí kam-goān,</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#8E403A>Beh lâi kā lí chim 3 kái!”</font>
<br>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>So it came to pass that Princess May Margret went to her bed a beauteous maiden, full of grace, and rose next morning a Laidly Worm; for when her tire-women came to dress her they found coiled up in her bed an awesome dragon, which uncoiled itself and came towards them. And when they ran away terrified, the Laidly Worm crawled and crept, and crept and crawled down to the sea till it reached the rock of the Spindlestone which is called the Heugh. And there it curled itself round the stone, and lay basking in the sun.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Tāi-chì to̍h án-ne發生--a，Mé Má-koeh公主e暗去睏ê時koh是1位美麗koh優雅ê少女，隔轉kang精神ê時soah變做1尾恐怖ê蛇龍；伊ê cha-bó͘使用人beh來kā伊梳妝打扮ê時chūn，去看tio̍h講眠床頂是1尾恐怖ê龍khûn tī hia，chit尾龍seng-khu放ho͘鬆--開ǹg in sô--去。Tī ta̍k-ke驚kah beh死做in走ê時，恐怖ê蛇龍to̍h sô--leh soan--leh，soan--leh sô--leh ta̍uh-ta̍uh-á sô ùi海邊á去，siōng尾sô到1塊在地號做Hiú ê紡錘形ê石頭hia。伊to̍h kā ka-tī khûn tī hit粒石頭頂，moa tī hia曝日。</font>

<font size=5 color=#3F48CC>He wrapped her in his mantle, for she trembled in the cold sea air, and carried her to Bamborough Castle, where the wicked Queen, knowing her hour was come, stood, all deserted by her imps and witch-wives, on the stairs, twisting her hands.</font>
