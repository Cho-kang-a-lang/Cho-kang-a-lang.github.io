<font size=5>Lán ê祖先有流傳1 ê傳說，in講天下萬物，m̄管是動物a̍h是植物，只要吸收日月精華，koh經過不斷修行，to̍h會使chiâⁿ做人。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Khah早有1隻虎，伊to̍h有án-ne ê願望，向望ka-tī ē-tàng有chiâⁿ做人ê hit 1日。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Chit 1日，伊hâm進前kāng款tiàm-tī山--lih teh修行，伊hiông-hiông想tio̍h講：「Ah！害ā，我to̍h ài趕緊koh食kúi-ê-á人ê gín-á ，nā無to̍h無法度繼續維持人形--ā!」窮實m̄免食gín-á，nā是用心修行，mā是會使chiâⁿ做人。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hit隻虎四界走chhōe，lo̍h-bóe來到1戶人家厝ê頭前，看tio̍h 1 ê婦人人kán-ná beh出門ê款，tng tī in tau ê門口埕kah 3 ê cha-po͘ gín-á teh講話。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>婦人人講：「好--ah！好--ah！阿母ài出門--ah！Lín今á日to̍h khah早睏--leh！E暗m̄-thang koh走出去chhit-thô，有聽tio̍h--無？」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>細漢小弟阿勳jû講：「我chiah無ài hiah早睏--leh！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿母ná so阿勳ê頭ná講：「阿勳，你nā是乖乖聽話，明á載阿母to̍h買你上ài食ê李á糖hō͘你食，好--無？」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿勳聽tio̍h有李á糖thang食，sûi tio̍h吞嘴nōa，bē輸iau鬼附身，歡頭喜面應講：「好--ā！好--ā！有李á糖thang食--ah！有李á糖thang食--ah！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>二兄阿民kā in阿母ê手牽--leh，問講：「阿母，你tang時chiah會轉--來？」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿母mā kā阿民ê手牽--leh，應講：「阿民，你乖，阿媽破病，lín阿爸已經先去照顧阿媽--ah！阿母mā ài趕緊過--去。明á載過晝lín阿爸to̍h會轉來。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>大兄阿祥tǹg胸坎掛保證，臭屁講：「阿母，你免煩惱，小弟in我會照顧。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿母chiâⁿ安慰阿祥hiah bat代誌，手kā阿祥ê手kó͘ so-so--leh講：「阿祥上乖--ah！按ne 2 ê小弟to̍h交代hō͘--你-ah-o͘h！」Sòa--落-來oa̍t頭kā阿勳講：「尤其是阿勳，你m̄-thang hiah狡怪，ài乖乖聽阿兄ê話。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hông點名ê阿勳，面臭臭koh kek小鬼á面。1點á to̍h無認同in阿母ê話。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿母看--tio̍h，無奈搖頭koh hoah 1聲：「阿勳--ah...。」To̍h kā 3 ê gín-á koh吩咐1擺，講：「Lín聽ho͘好勢--o͘h！等--leh阿母出門了後，lín to̍h kā門chhòaⁿ--leh，千萬bē-tàng kā生份人開門。Nā無，he人teh風聲ê虎姑婆可能會趁機會chông--入-來-o͘h！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>3 ê gín-á聽in阿母按ne講，lóng驚ka̍h。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿民chiok驚hiâⁿ問講：「虎姑婆kám會kā goán食--去？」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿母笑笑á講：「Mài驚！Mài驚！He kan-na是人teh風聲--ê niā-niā。Lín nā是乖乖á聽阿母ê話，bàng kā生份人開門，歹人to̍h bē入--來-ah！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿勳雙手tēⁿ拳頭拇，koh kek kah chiok chhàng-chhiu，ná beh kâng phah ê形講：「Hńg！我chiah m̄驚虎姑婆--leh！伊nā是敢來，我定tio̍h ka phah kah黏tī土腳peh bē--起-來。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>聽阿勳按ne chhiàng聲koh兼帶動作，ta̍k-ke lóng笑kah嘴á li̍h-sai-sai。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿祥看天色漸漸暗--loài，提醒in阿母講：「阿母！天暗--ah-lah！你趕緊去阿媽hia，bàng siuⁿ òaⁿ出門--lah！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿母起身前koh chài 1擺叮嚀講：「好！阿母beh來去--ah！Lín緊入--去，kā門chhòaⁿ--leh！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿母翻頭to̍h行出門，3 ê cha-po͘ gín-á mā翻頭行入去厝內，kā門chhòaⁿ--khaih。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hit隻想beh chiâⁿ做人ê虎一直bih tī邊--á，kā in母á kiáⁿ ê對話聽kah清清楚楚。看tio̍h in阿母離開厝了後，想想--leh，講：「有--ah！我想tio̍h 1 ê不止á好ê辦法--ah！」Sòa--落-來伊chiū kā ka-tī變做1 ê老大人，頭殼頂pa̍k 1條黃gìm-gìm ê頭巾，身軀moa1領烏mà-mà ê風moa。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>E暗月娘koân-koân tī天頂，阿勳有夠gia̍t--ê，tī厝--lih chông來chông去，無1時定tio̍h。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿祥看--tio̍h to̍h大聲ka hoah講：「阿勳，bàng hia chhia熊pōaⁿ獅--a，khah乖--leh！緊去眠床睏。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿勳kek小鬼á面ka應講：「無ài，我chit-má精神tng好，睏bē去。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿祥kā阿勳heh驚講：「你koh m̄聽話，阿母nā是知影你無乖，你to̍h無李á糖thang食--ah！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿民mā tàu苦勸阿勳講：「Hèⁿ--lah！阿勳，你乖乖á聽阿兄ê話--lah！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿勳氣phut-phut講：「Hńg！你定tio̍h koh beh kā阿母tâu，害我無李á糖thang食，tio̍h--無？」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Chit ê時陣，外口傳來lòng門ê聲，sòa--落-來to̍h聽tio̍h 1 ê老大人ê聲講：「我是姑婆，我是姑婆，緊開門。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿勳聽外口ê人講伊是姑婆，to̍h想beh去開門。阿祥ka阻擋，koh憢疑憢疑問外口ê人，講：「姑婆？你是tó 1 ê姑婆？你ê聲thài會聽--起-來kán-ná怪怪？」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>虎姑婆sûi嗽幾ā聲，koh笑hai-hai應講：「A-to̍h姑婆進前身體一直kò-iūⁿ，to̍h lóng無才調來看--lín。Tú chiah lín阿母bē放心，專工去拜托我來看顧lín。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>講soah koh嗽幾ā聲，chiah koh繼續講：「Lín ka聽看māi，我嗽kah lóng sau聲--ah！莫怪聲音ē怪怪。Ai-io͘h！外口chiok寒--ê neh！趕緊hō͘姑婆入去--lah！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿勳gia̍h椅á kóe腳ùi窗á ka看--出-去，憢疑憢疑kā外口ê人問講：「Ĕ！姑婆你nah會hiah膨皮？你tú chiah m̄是講你身體無好？」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>虎姑婆心內想講：「你這ê猴gín-á，正經有gia̍t--ê。」苦笑ka應講：「Hèⁿ--ah！可能是chit-chām-á破病食kài chē補，無sè-jī to̍h ke khah hàng-lin--ah！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>虎姑婆ná kā阿勳應話心內ná teh想代誌，soah無張持kā ka-tī ê心思講--出-來：「Gín-á兄，我ê腹肚chiok iau--ê！」伊sûi to̍h發見ka-tī講m̄-tio̍h--去，緊轉嘴講：「M̄是--lah！阿勳，緊hō͘姑婆入去歇睏。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>厝--lih 3 ê兄弟互相siòng過來siòng過去，2 ê大漢--ê同齊kā阿勳搖頭，阿勳koh kā虎姑婆講：「我感覺你kài奇怪，我chiah無ài hō͘你入--來。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>虎姑婆koh嗽幾ā聲，假做chiâⁿ有疼心koh親切ê款，笑笑á講：「Lín阿母講--ê，果然無m̄-tio̍h。阿勳上gia̍t--ā！你mài koh hâm姑婆講耍笑--ah！M̄-thang hō͘阿祥操煩--lah！姑婆有chah lín上ài食ê李á糖，beh來hō͘ lín食--o͘h！」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿勳聽tio̍h有李á糖thang食，嘴nōa chha̍p-chha̍p tin，講：「姑婆，你有chah李á糖來o͘h！」Koh oa̍t頭kā 2 ê兄哥講：「阿兄，伊正實是姑婆，伊hâm lán ê名lóng知--neh！」講soah to̍h走去kā門開--開，hō͘虎姑婆 --入-來，阿祥kah阿民根本to̍h bē赴擋。虎姑婆chiah入門，阿民to̍h驚kah走轉去房間bih，留阿祥、阿勳kah虎姑婆tī客廳。</font>
<br>

<font size=5>阿祥看tio̍h虎姑婆行--入-來，sui-bóng無認同，不而過mā是chiâⁿ乖巧ka叫1聲姑婆。虎姑婆應講：「乖！阿祥chiâⁿ乖。」</font>
<br>

<font size=5>虎姑婆入門了後會hâm阿祥in 3 ê兄弟發生siáⁿ物代誌--leh？請逐家後禮拜來ka看māi--leh-o͘h！</font>

<font size=5>Lán ê chó͘-sian ū liû-thoân 1 ê thoân-soat, in kóng thian-hā bān-bu̍t, m̄-koán sī tōng-bu̍t a̍h-sī si̍t-bu̍t, chí-iàu khip-siu ji̍t-goa̍t cheng-hôa, koh keng-kòe put-toān siu-hēng, to̍h ē-sái chiâⁿ-chò lâng.</font>
<br><br><p>

<font size=5>Khah chá ū 1 chiah hó͘, i to̍h ū án-ne ê goān-bōng, ǹg-bāng ka-tī ē-tàng ū chiâⁿ-chò lâng ê hit 1 ji̍t.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Chit 1 ji̍t, i hâm chìn-chêng kāng-khoán tiàm-tī soaⁿ--lih teh siu-hēng, i hiông-hiông siūⁿ-tio̍h kóng: “Ah! Hāi-ā, góa ài kóaⁿ-kín koh chia̍h kúi-ê-á lâng ê gín-á, nā-bô to̍h bô-hoat-tō͘ kè-sio̍k î-chhî lâng-hêng--ā!” Khêng-si̍t m̄-bián chia̍h gín-á, nā-sī iōng-sim siu-hēng, mā-sī ē-sái chiâⁿ-chò lâng.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hit chiah hó͘ sì-kè cháu-chhōe, lo̍h-bóe lâi-kàu 1 hō͘ jîn-ke-chhù ê thâu-chêng, khòaⁿ-tio̍h 1 ê hū-jîn-lâng kán-ná beh chhut-mn̂g ê khoán, tng tī in tau ê mn̂g-kháu-tiâⁿ kah 3 ê cha-po͘ gín-á teh kóng-ōe. </font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hū-jîn-lâng kóng: “Hó--ah! Hó--ah! A-bú ài chhut-mn̂g--ah! Lín kin-á-ji̍t to̍h khah-chá khùn--leh! E-àm m̄-thang koh cháu chhut-khì chhit-thô, ū thiaⁿ-tio̍h--bô?”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Sè-hàn sió-tī A-hun jû kóng: ”Góa chiah bô-ài hiah chá khùn--leh!”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-bú ná so A-hun ê thâu ná kóng: “A-hun, lí nā-sī koai-koai thiaⁿ-ōe, bîn-á-chài A-bú to̍h bé lí siōng-ài chia̍h ê lí-á-thn̂g hō͘ lí chia̍h, hó--bô?”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-hun thiaⁿ-tio̍h ū lí-á-thn̂g thang chia̍h, sûi tio̍h thun chhùi-nōa, bē-su iau-kúi hū-sin, hoaⁿ-thâu-hí-bīn ìn kóng: “Hó--ā! Hó--ā! Ū lí-á-thn̂g thang chia̍h--ah! Ū lí-á-thn̂g thang chia̍h--ah!” </font>
<br>

<font size=5>Jī-hiaⁿ A-bîn kā in a-bú ê chhiú khan--leh, mn̄g kóng: “A-bú, lí tang-sî chiah ē tńg--lâi?”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-bú mā kā A-bîn ê chhiú khan--leh, ìn kóng: “A-bîn, lí koai, a-má phòa-pēⁿ, lín a-pah í-keng seng khì chiàu-kò͘ a-má--ah! A-bú mā ài kóaⁿ-kín kòe--khì. Bîn-á-chài kòe-tàu lín a-pah to̍h ē tńg-lâi.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Tōa-hiaⁿ A-siông tǹg-heng-khám kòa pó-chèng, chhàu-phùi kóng: “A-bú, lí bián hoan-ló, sió-tī in góa ē chiàu-kò͘.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-bú chiâⁿ an-ùi A-siông hia bat tāi-chì, chhiú kā A-siông ê chhiú-kó͘ so-so--leh kóng：”A-siông siōng koai--ah! Án-ne 2 ê sió-tī to̍h kau-tāi hō͘--lí-ah-o͘h!” Sòa--lo̍h-lâi oa̍t-thâu kā A-hun kóng: ”Iû-kî sī A-hun, lí m̄-thang hiah káu-koài, ài koai-koai thiaⁿ A-hiaⁿ ê ōe.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hông tiám-miâ ê A-hun, bīn-chhàu-chhàu koh kek siáu-kúi-á-bīn, 1 tiám-á to̍h bô jīn-tông in a-bú ê ōe.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-bú khòaⁿ--tio̍h, bô-nāi iô-thâu koh hoah 1 siaⁿ: “A-hun--ah……” To̍h kā 3 ê gín-á koh hoan-hù 1 pái, kóng: “Lín thiaⁿ ho͘ hó-sè--o͘h! Tán--leh a-bú chhut-mn̂g liáu-āu, lín to̍h kā mn̂g chhòaⁿ--leh, chhian-bān bē-tàng kā seⁿ-hūn-lâng khui-mn̂g. Nā-bô, he lâng teh hong-siaⁿ ê Hó͘-ko͘-pô khó-lêng ē thàn ki-hōe chông--ji̍p-lâi-o͘h!.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>3 ê gín-á thiaⁿ in a-bú án-ne kóng, lóng kiaⁿ--ka̍h.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-bîn chiok kiaⁿ-hiâⁿ mn̄g kóng: “Hó͘-ko͘-pô kám ē kā goán chia̍h--khì?”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-bú chhiò-chhiò-á kóng: “Mài-kiaⁿ! Mài-kiaⁿ! He kan-na sī lâng teh hong-siaⁿ--ê niā-niā. Lín nā-sī koai-koai-á thiaⁿ a-bú ê ōe, bàng kā seⁿ-hūn-lâng khui-mn̂g, pháiⁿ-lâng to̍h bē ji̍p--lâi-ah!”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-hun siang-chhiú tēⁿ kûn-thâu-bó, koh kek kah chiok chhàng-chhiu, ná beh kâng phah ê hêng kóng: “Hńg! Góa chiah m̄-kiaⁿ Hó͘-ko͘-pô--leh! I nā-sī káⁿ lâi, góa tiāⁿ-tio̍h ka phah kah liâm tī thô͘-kha peh bē--khí-lâi.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Thiaⁿ A-hun án-ne chhiàng-siaⁿ koh kiam tài tōng-chok, ta̍k-ke lóng chhiò kah chhùi-á li̍h-sai-sai.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-siông khòaⁿ thiⁿ-sek chiām-chiām àm--loài, thê-chhéⁿ in a-bú kóng: "A-bú! Thiⁿ àm--ah-lah! Lí kóaⁿ-kín khì a-má hia, bàng siuⁿ òaⁿ chhut-mn̂g--lah!”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-bú khí-sin chêng koh chài 1 pái teng-lêng kóng: “Hó! A-bú beh lâi-khì--ah! Lín kín ji̍p--khì, kā mn̂g chhòaⁿ--leh.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-bú hoan-thâu to̍h kiâⁿ chhut-mn̂g, 3 ê cha-po͘ gín-á mā hoan-thâu kiâⁿ ji̍p-khì chhù-lāi, kā mn̂g chhòaⁿ--khaih.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hit chiah siūⁿ-beh chiâⁿ-chò lâng ê hó͘ it-ti̍t bih tī piⁿ--á, kā in  bó-á-kiáⁿ ê tùi-ōe thiaⁿ kah chheng-chheng-chhó-chhó. Khòaⁿ-tio̍h in a-bú lī-khui chhù liáu-āu, siūⁿ-siūⁿ--leh kóng: “Ū--ah! Góa siūⁿ-tio̍h 1 ê put-chí-á hó ê pān-hoat--ah.” Sòa--lo̍h-lâi i chiū kā ka-tī piàn-chò 1 ê lāu-tōa-lâng, thâu-khak-téng pa̍k 1 tiâu n̂g-gìm-gìm ê thâu-kin, seng-khu moa 1 niá o͘-mà-mà ê hong-moa.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>E-àm goe̍h-niû koân-koân tī thiⁿ-téng, A-hun ū-kàu gia̍t--ê, tī chhù--lih chông-lâi-chông-khì, bô 1 sî tiāⁿ-tio̍h.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-siông khòaⁿ--tio̍h to̍h tōa-siaⁿ ka hoah kóng: ”A-hun, bàng hia chhia-hîm pōaⁿ-sai--a, khah koai--leh! Kín khì bîn-chhn̂g khùn.” </font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-hun kek siáu-kúi-á-bīn ka ìn kóng: “Bô ài, góa chit-má cheng-sîn tng hó, khùn bē khì.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-siông kā A-hun heh-kiaⁿ kóng: ”Lí koh m̄ thiaⁿ-ōe, a-bú nā-sī chai-iáⁿ lí bô koai, lí to̍h bô lí-á-thn̂g thang chia̍h--ah.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-bîn mā tàu khó͘-khǹg A-hun kóng: “Hèⁿ--lah! A-hun, lí koai-koai-á thiaⁿ A-hiaⁿ ê ōe--lah!”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-hun khì-phut-phut kóng: “Hńg! Lí tiāⁿ-tio̍h koh beh kā a-bú tâu,  hāi-góa bô lí-á-thn̂g thang chia̍h, tio̍h--bô?”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Chit ê sî-chūn, gōa-kháu thoân-lâi lòng-mn̂g ê siaⁿ, sòa--lo̍h-lâi to̍h thiaⁿ-tio̍h 1 ê lāu-tōa-lâng ê siaⁿ kóng: “Góa sī ko͘-pô, góa sī ko͘-pô, kín khui-mn̂g.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-hun thiaⁿ gōa-kháu ê lâng kóng i sī ko͘-pô, to̍h siūⁿ-beh khì khui-mn̂g. A-siông ka chó͘-tòng, koh giâu-gî giâu-gî mn̄g gōa-kháu ê lâng, kóng: “Ko͘-pô? Lí sī tó 1 ê ko͘-pô? Lí ê siaⁿ thài ē thiaⁿ--khí-lâi kán-ná koài-koài?”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hó͘-ko͘-pô sûi sàu kúi-ā siaⁿ, koh chhiò-hai-hai ìn-kóng: “A-to̍h ko͘-pô chìn-chêng sin-thé it-ti̍t kò-iūⁿ, to̍h lóng bô châi-tiāu lâi khòaⁿ--lín. Tú-chiah lín a-bú bē hòng-sim, choan-kang khì pài-thok góa lâi khòaⁿ-kò͘ lín.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Kóng soah koh sàu kúi-ā siaⁿ, chiah koh kè-sio̍k kóng: “Lín ka thiaⁿ khòaⁿ-māi, góa sàu kah lóng sau-siaⁿ--ah! Bo̍k-koài siaⁿ-im ē koài-koài. Ai-ioh! Gōa-kháu chiok kôaⁿ--ê-neh! Kóaⁿ-kín hō͘ ko͘-pô ji̍p-khì--lah!”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-hun gia̍h í-á kóe-kha ùi thang-á ka khòaⁿ--chhut-khì, giâu-gî giâu-gî kā gōa-kháu ê lâng mn̄g kóng: “Ĕ! Ko͘-pô lí nah-ē hiah phòng-phôe? Lí tú-chiah m̄-sī kóng lí sin-thé bô-hó?”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hó͘-ko͘-pô sim-lāi siūⁿ-kóng: “Lí chit ê kâu-gín-á, chèng-keng ū gia̍t--ê.” Khó͘-chhiò ka ìn-kóng: “Hèⁿ--ah! Khó-lêng sī chit-chām-á phòa-pēⁿ chia̍h kài chē pó͘, bô-sè-jī to̍h ke khah hàng-lin--ah!“ </font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hó͘-ko͘-pô ná kā A-hun ìn-ōe sim-lāi ná teh siūⁿ tāi-chì, soah bô-tiuⁿ-tî kā ka-tī ê sim-sū kóng--chhut-lâi: “Gín-á-hiaⁿ, góa ê pak-tó͘ chiok iau--ê!” I sûi to̍h hoat-kiàn ka-tī m̄-tio̍h--khì, kín  tńg-chhùi kóng: “M̄-sī--lah! A-hun, kín hō͘ ko͘-pô ji̍p-khì hioh-khùn.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Chhù--lih 3 ê hiaⁿ-tī hō͘-siong siòng kòe-lâi siòng kòe-khì, 2 ê tōa-hàn--ê tâng-chê kā A-hun iô-thâu, A-hun koh kā Hó͘-ko͘-pô kóng: “Góa kám-kak lí kài kî-koài, góa chiah bô-ài hō͘ lí ji̍p--lâi.”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>Hó͘-ko͘-pô koh sàu kúi-ā siaⁿ, ké-chò chiâⁿ ū thiàⁿ-sim koh chhin-chhiat ê khoán, chhiò-chhiò-á kóng: “Lín a-bú kóng--ê, kó-jiân bô m̄-tio̍h. A-hun siōng gia̍t--ā! Lí mài koh hâm ko͘-pô kóng-sńg-chhiò--ah! M̄-thang hō͘ A-siông chhau-hoân--lah! Ko͘-pô ū chah lín siōng-ài chia̍h ê lí-á-thn̂g, beh lâi hō͘ lín chia̍h--o͘h!”</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-hun thiaⁿ-tio̍h ū lí-á-thn̂g thang chia̍h, chhùi-nōa chha̍p-chha̍p tin kóng: “Ko͘-pô, lí ū chah lí-á-thn̂g lâi o͘h!” Koh oa̍t-thâu kā 2 ê hiaⁿ-ko kóng: ”A-hiaⁿ, i chiâⁿ-si̍t sī ko͘-pô, i hâm lán ê miâ lóng chai--neh!” Kóng soah to̍h cháu khì kā mn̂g khui--khui, hō͘ Hó͘-ko͘-pô --ji̍p-lâi, A-siông kah A-bîn kin-pún to̍h bē-hù tòng. Hó͘-ko͘-pô chiah ji̍p-mn̂g, A-bîn to̍h kiâⁿ kah cháu tńg-khì pâng-keng bih, lâu A-siông, A-hun kah Hó͘-ko͘-pô tī kheh-thiaⁿ.</font>
<br>

<font size=5>A-siông khòaⁿ-tio̍h Hó͘-ko͘-pô kiâⁿ--ji̍p-lâi, sui-bóng bô jīn-tông, put-jî-kò mā-sī chiâⁿ koai-khá ka kiò 1 siaⁿ ko͘-pô. Hó͘-ko͘-pô ìn kóng: “Koai! A-siông chiâⁿ koai.”</font>
